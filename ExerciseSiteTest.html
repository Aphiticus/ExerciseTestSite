Best Use Notes
  • Walking and running work best with the phone in a pocket, waistband, or arm band.
  • Pushups and situps work best when the phone position stays consistent for the entire set.
  • iPhone requires tapping the permission button before motion data is available.
  • This is a browser estimate, not medical or gym-grade measurement.
On iPhone Safari and many mobile browsers, this page must be opened from HTTPS or localhost for sensor access. Background counting is limited in browsers, so keep the page open and screen active during tests.
